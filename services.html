Services Offered

24 Hours Emergency Service

Emergency medical services (EMS), also known as paramedic services, are emergency services that provide urgent pre-hospital treatment and stabilisation for serious illness and injuries and transport to definitive care. Doctors and medical staffs at Sunsari Community Hospital provide emergency services 24 hours a day. Most people go to a hospital emergency room when they have been in a serious accident. They also do so when they have broken bones or other body parts that are out of place. Regardless of their justification for visiting an emergency room, most patients require immediate attention in order to capitalize on the opportunity window that can save their lives.

OPD from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM

OPD means an Out Patient Department of a hospital. It is the section of the hospital where patients are provided medical consultations and other allied services.

We have following parts and services.

  • Consultation chambers where patients are provided medical, surgical or allied consultation and expert opinion.
  • Examination rooms where patients can be examined for any disease condition.
  • Diagnostics which have radiology, pathology,microbiology and other diagnostic services and/or sample collection point.
  • Pharmacy which provides medications to the patients.

Sunsari Community Hospital provides stationary ECG service. This service is dynamic and is not a appointment organized. 24 Hours Digital X-Ray Services

We provide 24 hrs x-ray service to our patients. X-rays are a type of radiation called electromagnetic waves. X-ray imaging creates pictures of the inside of your body. The images show the parts of your body in different shades of black and white. This is because different tissues absorb different amounts of radiation. Patients can get their body parts scanned and can get treatment in a better manner.

24 Hours Lab Services

We also have service of 24 hours pathalogy lab. The delivery of a specimen to the pathology laboratory initiates a complex series of events resulting in a pathologic diagnosis/interpretation. It performs diagnostic routine and special tests. Certified technicians perform the tests with state-of- the-art automated analyzers. They perform manual microscopic and analytical procedures necessary to provide accurate result. It provides round the clock emergency service including point of care testing (POCT).

12 Hours Dental Services

At Sunsari Community Hospital we provide quality dental service. A general dentist is our primary care dental provider. Our dentist diagnoses, treats, and manages your overall oral health care needs including gum care, root canals, fillings, crowns, veneers, bridges(Metal Ceramic Birdge, Zirkonia Birdge, E-Max Bridge), dental x-ray, Fix Ortho, Removal Ortho, RPD (Removal Partial Denture), FPD (Full Partial Denture), scaling, and preventive education.
