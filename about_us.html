History

Sunsari Community Hospital (Pvt.) Ltd. is one of the oldest and most trusted hospital in this area. It is located in Duhabi, one of the major industrial area of eastern part of Nepal. This Hospital offers preventive and curative healthcare services specially targeting the eastern region of country.

Established in the year 2004 A.D this hospital is based on the principle of “Swastha Nepali-Bikashit Nepal”. Sunsari Community Hospital, recognized by Nepal Medical Council, Ministry of Health has constantly maintained its standard as per the requirement of other Medical Council and Hospitals across the eastern part of Nepal. This hospital has various modern medicinal equipment’s which is used to perform various tests for serving the patients.

Mission And Vision

The mission of Sunsari Community Hospital is to provide a responsive healing environment for patients and their families, and to improve the quality of life for all members of our community. We aim to be a provider of high quality patient-focused health care that is readily accessible, cost effective and meets the needs of the communities we serve. We excel at providing for the healthcare needs of our region through quality, efficient and patient-centered care.
